OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) refers to collecting publicly available information about a target.

---
### Types of OSINT

1. **Passive OSINT** – No direct interaction with target.
    - Examples: Social media, public websites, Google search.
2. **Active OSINT** – Direct interaction with target systems.
    - Examples: WHOIS lookup, DNS queries.
---
### Common Tools and Sites
- https://hunter.io – Find email addresses
- https://www.dehashed.com – Check leaked credentials
- https://clearbit.com – Company data
- https://haveibeenpwned.com – Breach check
- https://censys.io – Internet scanning data
---
### WHOIS Lookup Commands
```bash
whois example.com                            # Get domain registration info                                                     (owner, registrar, creation/expiry)
whois amazon.com                             # Check who owns amazon.com
whois 192.168.1.1                            # Works with IPs to identify                                                       allocation info
```
