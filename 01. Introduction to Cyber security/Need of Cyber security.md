- **Protect Personal Information**  
    To keep your passwords, identity, and private data safe from hackers.
    
- **Ensure Business Security**  
    To prevent data breaches and keep companies running without cyber disruptions.
    
- **Secure Financial Transactions**  
    To protect online payments and banking from fraud and theft.
    
- **Follow Legal Compliance**  
    To meet laws like GDPR, HIPAA, and avoid legal penalties for data leaks.
    
- **Prevent Cybercrimes**  
    To stop threats like hacking, phishing, and online scams.
    
- **Protect Critical Infrastructure**  
    To defend essential services like hospitals and electricity from attacks.
    
- **Maintain Trust and Reputation**  
    To build customer confidence by showing data is handled securely.
