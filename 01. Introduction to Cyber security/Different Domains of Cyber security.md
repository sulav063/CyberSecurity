- **Network Security**: Protects data during transmission across networks from unauthorized access or attacks.
    
- **Application Security**: Secures software applications by finding and fixing vulnerabilities in their code.
    
- **Cloud Security**: Involves protecting data, applications, and services hosted in cloud environments.
    
- **Information Security**: Focuses on safeguarding digital and physical data from unauthorised access or alteration.
    
- **Operational Security (OpSec)**: Deals with managing and protecting critical processes, user permissions, and procedures.
    
- **Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity**: Ensures systems can recover and continue functioning after a cyberattack or failure.
    
- **End-user Education**: Involves training users to recognize and avoid security threats like phishing and social engineering.
    
- **Ethical Hacking / Offensive Security**: Uses hacking skills legally to identify and fix security weaknesses before malicious hackers do.
