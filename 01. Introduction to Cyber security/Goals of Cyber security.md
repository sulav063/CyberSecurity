#### CIA Triad:
#### C-Confidentiality
Keep information secret.
Example: Your Facebook messages are private and only you can read them.
****
#### I-Integrity
Keep information correct and unchanged.
Example: A bank transfer of ₹100 should not suddenly change to ₹10,000.
****
#### A-Availability
Make sure information is available when needed.
Example: You can log into your email anytime without the server being down
