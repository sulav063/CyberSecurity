## GRC - Governance, Risk, and Compliance
**1. ISO Compliance (ISO/IEC 27001)**
	ISO/IEC 27001 is an international standard that outlines best practices for managing information security.
	Example:  
	A tech company follows ISO 27001 to ensure that customer data is stored securely and access is well controlled.
**2. HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)**
	A U.S. law that protects personal health information (PHI) from being shared without consent.
	Example:  
	A hospital encrypts patient records and allows access only to doctors involved in the treatment.
**3. PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)**
	A global standard for securing credit and debit card transactions.
	Example:  
	An e-commerce website uses encryption and secure servers to protect your card details during online payments
**4. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**
	A European Union law that gives individuals control over their personal data and requires businesses to protect it.
	Example:  
	A website must ask for your consent before collecting cookies and allow you to delete your data if you request it.
